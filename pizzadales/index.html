Pizza Dales

Pizza Dales is the flagship universe of SterlWorld — a fast‑paced, character‑driven world built around attitude, chaos, and momentum. Focused on expressive animation, tight gameplay, and 90s‑inspired energy, Pizza Dales blends humor, challenge, and personality into a growing arcade universe.

At the center is Dale — a rebellious, high‑energy dingo navigating school life, rivals, and nonstop action. Every character and mechanic is designed to feel alive, reactive, and fun to play.

Pizza Dales: Detention Dash

Available Now on the App Store!

Pizza Dales: Detention Dash is the first official release in the Pizza Dales universe — a fast‑paced, hand‑drawn endless runner where Dale escapes detention and outruns his rival, Rocket.

Sprint through the halls of Yokey Oats High as you dodge obstacles, collect tokens, and maintain momentum. Every run is a test of reflexes, flow, and timing.

Built for quick sessions and tight controls, Detention Dash delivers expressive animation, character‑driven gameplay, and the signature attitude of the Pizza Dales universe.
