Pizza Dales

Pizza Dales is the flagship universe of SterlWorld — a fast-paced, character-driven world centered around attitude, chaos, and momentum. Built with a strong focus on gameplay-first design and personality, Pizza Dales blends humor, challenge, and expressive animation into a unique arcade experience.

At the center of it all is Dale — a rebellious, high-energy dingo navigating school life, rivals, and nonstop action. Every character, mechanic, and moment is designed to feel alive, reactive, and fun to play.

Pizza Dales: Detention Dash

Pizza Dales: Detention Dash is the first official release in the Pizza Dales universe — a momentum-based endless runner built for mobile.

Starting in detention, players take control of Dale as he escapes into a fast-paced run through Yokey Oats High School, avoiding obstacles, collecting tokens, and staying ahead of Rocket — the relentless rival always close behind.

Designed for quick sessions and tight controls, Detention Dash focuses on flow, reaction, and character-driven gameplay.

Launching on the App Store this April!
