About SterlWorld

SterlWorld is an independent multimedia studio founded by Sterlin Griffin, focused on building original games, characters, and connected interactive worlds.

Built around strong identity, expressive gameplay, and memorable characters, SterlWorld develops original intellectual properties spanning games, animation, storytelling, and interactive media.

The studio’s flagship universe, Pizza Dales , introduces a fast-paced, character-driven arcade world centered around momentum, humor, rivalry, and chaos.

SterlWorld’s first official release, Pizza Dales: Detention Dash , is available now on the App Store, bringing players into the halls of Yokey Oats High as they outrun Rocket and survive the nonstop energy of the Pizza Dales universe.

Every project within SterlWorld is designed with a focus on gameplay-first experiences, recognizable visual identity, emotional energy, and long-term worldbuilding.

As the universe expands, SterlWorld will continue growing into a larger connected ecosystem of original entertainment properties and interactive experiences.
