About SterlWorld

SterlWorld is an independent game and multimedia studio founded by Sterlin Griffin, focused on creating original games, characters, and interactive experiences.

The studio is currently developing its flagship title, Pizza Dales: Detention Dash, a fast-paced mobile game that introduces players to the world of Pizza Dales and its characters.

SterlWorld is built on a long-term vision of creating a connected universe of original intellectual properties spanning games, animation, storytelling, and interactive media.

Each project is designed with a focus on strong identity, memorable characters, and gameplay-driven experiences that feel expressive, engaging, and distinct.

While currently operated as an independent studio, SterlWorld is actively growing toward becoming a full-scale multimedia brand.

This is only the beginning.
